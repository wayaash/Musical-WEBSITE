What is this?

This is a You Ship, We Ship (YSWS) program by Hack Club.

You build any project based on music — apps, websites, tools, visualizers, instruments, or experiments.

As you log hours using Hackatime, you unlock real music rewards.

Rules

Eligibility

  • 13–18 years old
  • Member of Hack Club Slack
  • Verified identity
  • Not banned from Hackatime

Project requirements

  • Must be music-centric
  • No AI-generated projects
  • Hackatime tracking required
  • Real progress expected

Rewards

  • 1 hr → Stickers
  • 5 hrs → Music album of your choice
  • 10 hrs → $50 microphone
  • 20 hrs → $100 headphones
  • 30 hrs → $150 speaker / soundbox
  • 40 hrs → Instrument (up to $200)
  • 50 hrs → Better instrument (up to $250)
